# AI Assistant

The AI Assistant is a context-aware chat panel that helps managers and team members with evaluations, self-assessments, and understanding skill data.

## Opening the Chat

Click the **chat bubble** button in the bottom-right corner of any page to open the AI Assistant panel. The panel is resizable by dragging its left edge.

## Screen-Aware Context

The AI Assistant automatically knows what you're viewing:

- **Dashboard**: Understands your team composition and stats
- **Skillset View**: Knows which skillset, assessment, team member, and tab you're looking at
- **Self-Evaluation**: Can guide you through rating yourself skill-by-skill

::: tip
The AI uses your screen context to give relevant answers. If you're viewing a specific team member's evaluation, the assistant will reference their data.
:::

## What You Can Ask

### For Managers
- "How is my team performing on this skillset?"
- "Who needs improvement in critical skills?"
- "Help me evaluate [member name]"
- "Explain the gap analysis results"

### For Users
- "Help me with my self-evaluation"
- "What does a score of 3 mean for this skill?"
- "How do my scores compare to the team average?"

## Conversations

- Conversations are saved per user and persist across sessions
- Use the search bar to find past conversations
- Create new conversations for different topics
- Conversations are auto-titled from your first message

## Privacy & Data Scoping

- **Managers** see data for their team members only
- **Users** can only access their own evaluation data
- The AI enforces role-based access — it cannot leak data across users
- GDPR-compliant data scoping is applied to every response
