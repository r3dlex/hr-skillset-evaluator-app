# User Workflow

A guide for team members being evaluated in the HR Skillset Evaluator.

## Step 1: View Your Scores

Log in and check your **Dashboard** to see an overview of your evaluations. The dashboard shows your stats across all skillsets.

## Step 2: Complete a Self-Evaluation

1. Click on a skillset from the sidebar
2. Click **Start self-evaluation** or navigate to the self-evaluation page
3. Rate yourself on each skill using the sliders (0–5)
4. Click **Save Scores**

::: tip AI Help
Click **Ask AI Assistant** on the self-evaluation page for guidance on how to rate yourself. The AI can explain what each score level means.
:::

## Step 3: View Your Radar Chart

After evaluations are submitted (by you or your manager), check the **Radar Chart** tab to see a visual representation of your skills.

## Step 4: Check Gap Analysis

The **Gap Analysis** tab shows differences between:
- Your **self-evaluation** and your **manager's evaluation**
- Your scores and the **team average**
- Your scores and the **role average** (others with the same job title)

::: info
A positive gap means your manager rated you higher than you rated yourself. A negative gap means the opposite — useful for identifying areas where perceptions differ.
:::

## Step 5: Track Progress Over Time

As new assessments are created (e.g., quarterly reviews), you can switch between them to track your growth.
